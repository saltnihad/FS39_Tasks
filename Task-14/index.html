ALDMERI DOMINION

Led by Queen Ayrenn of the High Elves, the Aldmeri Dominion seeks to restore Elven dominance in Tamriel. The alliance combines the ancient strength of the Altmer, the cunning of the Bosmer, and the mysterious nature of the Khajiit, aiming to preserve Elven heritage and reclaim power for the Elves.

DAGGERFALL COVENANT

The Daggerfall Covenant is a strategic alliance between the Bretons, Orcs, and Redguards. These three races unite to protect their homelands and assert their independence from Elven control. Together, they seek to restore order and protect the kingdom from both internal and external threats.

EBONHEART PACT

The Ebonheart Pact is formed by the Nords, Dark Elves, and Argonians, united by a desire to reclaim their lands and protect their people. With a focus on survival and strength, this alliance values its resilience and seeks to overcome historical oppression from other factions in Tamriel.
